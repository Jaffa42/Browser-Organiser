Flows are serieses of actions triggered by various conditions.

A flow will run when one of its triggers are met, and specifiers can be used to specify what it applies to.


You have no buttons

Buttons can be used to run flows with the 'button' condition from the sidebar. One button can run multiple flows: simply use the same name for the button on both.

About Permissions

  • Tabs - Allows your flows to read tab info, close and pin/unpin tabs.
  • Hide tabs - Allows your flows to hide/show tabs.
  • Notifications - Allows your flows to send you notifications.
  • Management - Allows your flows to change the theme, and allows the 'Manage flows' menu to list installed themes.
  • Storage - We store your flows locally on your device.

This addon uses cookies to save your flows locally to your device. These cookies contain information about the contents of the flows. More info.
