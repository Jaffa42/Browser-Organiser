About Permissions

  • Tabs - Allows your flows to read tab info, close and pin/unpin tabs.
  • Hide tabs - Allows your flows to hide/show tabs.
  • Notifications - Allows your flows to send you notifications.
  • Management - Allows your flows to change the theme, and allows the 'Manage flows' menu to list installed themes.
  • Storage - We store your flows locally on your device.

We only request the permissions that we absolutely need in order to make your flows work.
